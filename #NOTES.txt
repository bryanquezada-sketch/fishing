-- UI --
FISH STAMINA/HP - Must reduce to 0 to catch
LINE TENSION -  Represented by a bar on the right side of the screen
                Green/Yellow Zone: Safe zones where you can aggressively reel
                Red Zone: If the bar maxes out, the line snaps instantly and you lose the fish
                Distance: If you fail to match icons, the fish "stresses" and swims further away
                          If distance > rod's [LIMIT] (in meters), the fish escapes

-- EFFECTS --

STUN -  This is critical, it stops the fish from regenerating and prevents it from swimming further away
        if missed, the fish remains active, making the fight much longer or causing
        the line to break.
GIVE SLACK - Can be used any time to drop tension, but doing so when the fish isn'tension
             struggling, is a waste of time, gives fish time to recover or gain ground
