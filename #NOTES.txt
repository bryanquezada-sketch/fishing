-- EFFECTS --

A TICK is = 1 to 1.5 seconds

REEL IN - Deals standard DMG, raises TENSION (3% to 5%) per TICK while the skill is active.
BIG REEL IN - Deals massive DMG, raises about 5% to 8% per TICK while the skill is active. Can only sustain for about 4-6 seconds
                before you must stop or use a utility skill, otherwise you'll snap the line
                Big gamble, only to be used if tension is <50%. If you BIG REEL IN while at 70%
                tension, and the fish doesn't swap to a STAND FIRM/SLACK move quickly, player is
                forced to stop attacking entirely to let the tension decay, which lets the fish heal
STUN -  This is critical, it stops the fish from regenerating and prevents it from swimming further away
        if missed, the fish remains active, making the fight much longer or causing
        the line to break.
STAND FIRM LEFT/RIGHT - Reduced TENSION by about ~10% per second while preventing fish from healing
GIVE SLACK - Can be used any time to drop TENSION(30%), but doing so when the fish isn't
             STRUGGLING, is a waste of time, gives fish time to recover or gain ground.
             if used while STRUGGLING, it's a reset button. Fastest way to tank tension
             from 90% back down to 40%, but the fish will usually swim 5-10 meters away
             during the animation
