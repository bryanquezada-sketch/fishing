🎣 Game Design Doc: Sport Fishing MVP
1. World & Preparation (The Setup)
The Hunt: Players find circling seagulls to locate a "School."
Chumming: Use specific Chum to trigger a 45-minute "Frenzy."
Weighted Spawns:
Small (Fry): ~80%
Medium: ~10-15%
Gargantuan: 5-10%
Initial Hook: Must react immediately to the bite for initial DMG, or lose bait/hook.

2. Battle Mechanics (The "Simon Says" Loop)
Core Concept: Fast-paced rhythm reaction + Tug-of-War vs. a 5-Minute Hook Timer.
Prompt Reaction Table
Fish Status	Required Skill	Success Effect
Leaping	Big Reel In	MAX Damage
Pulling	Reel In	Normal Damage
Moving Left	Stand Firm Left	STUN (Stops HP Regen/Movement) + Lowers Tension
Moving Right	Stand Firm Right	STUN (Stops HP Regen/Movement) + Lowers Tension
Struggling	Give Slack	Massive Tension Reduction
The "Tick" System (1.0 – 1.5 Seconds)
Active Attacks: Skills are not toggles; you must SPAM the key during the prompt.
Animation Lock: Using a skill locks the player for 1-1.5s.
The Override: If the prompt changes mid-animation, player must hit the new key immediately to clip the old animation and avoid a penalty.
Grace Period: Zero. Wrong key = Instant 15% Tension Spike.

3. Tension & Distance (Fail Conditions)
Tension Bar: Starts at 0%.
Passive Gain: +1-2% per second.
Active Gain: Reeling adds tension even when correct.
Natural Decay: Drains slowly if idle, but fish heals and gains distance.
Distance (Meters): Fish "stresses" and swims away if prompts are missed or player is idle.
Loss Conditions:
Tension hits 100% (Snap).
Distance exceeds Rod Limit (Escape).
Hook Timer hits 0 (Despawn).

4. Balancing & Math (Standard Fish)
Action	Tension Change	HP Damage
Passive/Idle	+1-2% / sec	0% (Fish heals 1-2%/sec)
Reel In (Correct)	+3-5% / tick	~2-3%
Big Reel (Correct)	+8-12% / tick	~5-7%
Stand Firm (Correct)	-5-8% / tick	0% (Stops Regen)
Give Slack (Correct)	-15-20% / tick	0% (Fish gains ~5m)
Wrong Button	+15% Spike	0% (Fish Heals)

5. Economy & Progression
Size	HP	Fight Time	Sale Value
Small	18,000	1-2 Min	8-11 $
Medium	30,000	3-4 Min	18-22 $
Gargantuan	37,000+	5 Min (Max)	34-45+ $
Note: Tuna is worth ~55% of Marlin value.

6. Expansion Ideas (Post-MVP)
Talent Tree: Upgrade "Grace Periods," "Tension Resistance," or "High-Test Line."
Boat Upgrades: Sonar for finding schools or larger storage tanks.
The Haul: A mini-game navigating back to the docks while slowed by fish, avoiding "Pirate" NPCs.
Labor System: Stamina cost per catch to manage game economy.
